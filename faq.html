◟FAQ◞

What is rejoice?

Rejoice is a company, specializing in the field of VR software development.

What are the product types?

The company offers many different modules. Each contains a realistic, visual representation.

What is a module?

A module is a dedicated proprietary software, specially build for delivering an exclusive ocular enjoyment.

How does it work?

Every module can be seamlessly initiated, after being integrated with a VR headset. A step-by-step guide is included in all our products.

I want to be assured of the quality of a particular module. Can I have a demo?

Certainly. Although the significantly short duration, the fidelity is exactly the same.

Do I need a wi-fi connection to start a module?

There is no such restriction. A connection to the internet is required only for the download itself.

Can I share my module?

Modules are bound with your personal email address, and this link can't be undone.

How can I make a request?

All modules are named. The name can be viewed by a simple hover. After sending us the name/names to my_vr_choice@rejoice.io, you will receive your collect path.
